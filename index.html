About Down Syndrome

Down syndrome is a genetic condition caused by an extra copy of chromosome 21. People with Down syndrome can live full, independent lives with proper support and inclusion.

Quick Facts

  • 1 in 700 babies are born with Down syndrome
  • There are three types: Trisomy 21, Translocation, and Mosaic
  • People with Down syndrome attend school, work, and live independently
  • Life expectancy has increased to 60+ years
